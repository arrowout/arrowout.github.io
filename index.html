What Is Arrow Out?

Arrow Out is a minimalist unblock-style puzzle game built around one simple, satisfying idea: every arrow can only slide in the direction it points, and your job is to pull them all out of the board. Tap an arrow and it shoots off in its facing direction — but only if nothing is blocking its path. Clear the board by removing the arrows in exactly the right order.

It looks easy at first. Then the arrows start overlapping, crossing, and trapping each other, and suddenly every move matters. Arrow Out is the kind of game you pick up for "just one level" and put down twenty levels later.

How to Play Arrow Out

  1. Read the board. Each arrow points in one fixed direction — up, down, left, or right.
  2. Tap to fire. When you tap an arrow, it tries to slide out of the board along the way it points.
  3. Mind the blockers. If another arrow is in the path, the tapped arrow can't move. You have to clear the blocker first.
  4. Plan the order. Solving a level is all about sequence — which arrow comes out first, second, third?
  5. Clear the board. Remove every arrow to finish the level and unlock the next challenge.
